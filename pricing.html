Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our pricing? Check out some of the common questions we receive, or contact us for more information.


Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations. Please contact us with your details to learn more about our non-profit discounts.


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment schedules.


Is there a contract for maintenance plans?

Our maintenance plans require a minimum 3-month commitment, after which they continue on a month-to-month basis. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.


Do you offer refunds?

We don't typically offer refunds for completed work. However, we do have a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you to ensure you're happy with the final product.
